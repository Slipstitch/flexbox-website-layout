Hi, I'm Sheila

I am a Responsive Designer & Developer who is passionate about visual storytelling and the best user experience. When I'm not working, pursuing side projects or getting lost in coursework, podcasts, and continued study, I can be found on long walks, crocheting, or enjoying my family.

My passion: Next to coding of course, I love crocheting! It is quite versatile as an artistic expression. I tend to give most of my creations away as gifts. It has developed into designing crochet projects which has given me an idea for creating a new app.

I have a love of Beekeeping. My husband and I look forward to each beekeeping season and manage our four-hives down by Lake Waconia. We have a great time sharing this hobby. Every fall we harvest the honey, make wax candles, and share the spoils with family and friends.
